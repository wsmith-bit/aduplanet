Cost FAQs

Are prefab ADUs cheaper than custom builds?
Often, yes—prefab can reduce build time and change‑order risk. Compare full project totals including site work and fees.
What’s the cheapest ADU type?
Garage conversions typically start lowest because the structure exists, but utility upgrades can change the math.
How accurate is the calculator?
It’s a planning tool using typical 2025 assumptions. For a firm quote, request 3 builder bids.
Do ADUs increase property taxes?
Usually your assessed value goes up by the value of the improvements. Rules vary—check our Regulations guide and local assessor.
Can I finance ADU construction?
Yes—HELOCs, construction loans, and cash‑out refis are common. See our Financing guide.
