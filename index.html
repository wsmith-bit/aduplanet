Frequently asked

What ADU size fits a typical backyard?

Many lots can fit a 400–800 sf detached ADU, but setbacks, height, lot coverage, and utilities control the final envelope. Check your city’s setbacks and max size on the Regulations page.

Is a garage conversion cheaper than a new detached ADU?

Often, yes—existing foundations and utilities can lower costs. Scope still matters (seismic, slab, insulation, egress). Compare your options on Costs.

Prefab or custom: which is faster?

Prefab can compress the on-site timeline; custom can fit tricky lots and HOA rules. See pros/cons and a builder directory on Builders.

How do people finance an ADU?

Common paths are HELOC, cash-out refinance, construction loans, or FHA 203k. Compare speed, paperwork, and payments on Financing.

Will an ADU increase property taxes?

Yes, the improvement value gets assessed separately in many jurisdictions. We link to official resources and suggest verifying with your county assessor. {{verify locally}}

Can I rent out my ADU short-term?

Short-term rental rules vary widely and often differ from long-term ADU allowances. Always confirm with your city’s municipal code or planning portal. {{verify locally}}
