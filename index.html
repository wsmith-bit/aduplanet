Homepage FAQ

What’s the first step?

Establish a target budget on Costs, then verify your local rules in Regulations.

Can I build two ADUs?

Some states and cities allow two ADUs per lot. Check your state/city guide under Regulations.

Prefab or custom?

Prefab accelerates delivery and predictability; custom maximizes fit. Our Builders page compares approaches and connects you with teams.

How do I finance?

Compare HELOC, cash-out refi, and construction loans on Financing, including a monthly payment calculator.

Where do I request bids?

Use the RFQ form at /estimate. Include ZIP, ADU type, budget, stage, timeline, and email.
