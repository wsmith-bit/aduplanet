Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to build an ADU?

Most projects fall between $100k and $300k depending on size, site work, labor rates, and finishes. Prefab can reduce total timeline.

Do I need a permit to build an ADU?

Yes. Rules vary by city and state. Start with setbacks, height, and utility connections; many places now have streamlined ADU approval.

Can I rent out my ADU?

Often yes, for long-term tenants. Short-term rentals may have extra restrictions; check local ordinances.

Are prefab ADUs worth it?

They can reduce construction time, site disruption, and change orders, especially for standard layouts.

How do I pay for an ADU?

Common options include HELOCs, cash-out refinancing, renovation or construction loans, and local grants where available.
