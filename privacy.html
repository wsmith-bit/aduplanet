Privacy Policy

We explain what we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the choices you have. This policy applies to aduplanet.com and related services.

TL;DR: We use minimal data to run our site, improve tools, and connect you with ADU professionals when you ask us to. You control your data—access, delete, or opt-out anytime.

What We Collect

Site usage
Pages viewed, clicks, and device/browser info (via privacy-conscious analytics).
Calculator inputs
ADU size, finish level, site complexity, fees, contingency and similar—stored as non-personal to improve ranges and averages.
RFQ details
ZIP, ADU type, budget, stage, timeline, email, optional phone, and notes—used to match you with builders or lenders only when you submit /estimate.
Contact messages
Questions you send to us (and our replies).
Cookies
Essential cookies for site function; optional analytics cookies (see Cookies).

How We Use Data

  • To operate the website, calculators, and forms.
  • To respond to inquiries and RFQs and to provide requested matches to vetted partners.
  • To analyze aggregate usage and improve copy, tools, and performance.
  • To prevent fraud and comply with law.

How We Share Data

  • Builder/Lender partners (by your request): RFQ details are shared with matched partners so they can contact you about your project.
  • Service providers: Hosting, email delivery, and analytics vendors process data on our behalf under contracts.
  • Legal: If required by law or to protect rights, safety, and integrity.

We do not sell personal information for money. Under the CCPA/CPRA, certain disclosures may be considered a "share" for targeted advertising. You can opt out below.
