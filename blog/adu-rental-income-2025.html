Find true comps (not fantasy comps)

  1. Match unit type (studio vs 1-bed) and square footage (¬±10‚Äì15%).
  2. Match location (same neighborhood or school zone; avoid freeway/noise outliers).
  3. Check features: parking, laundry, AC/heat type, outdoor space, privacy from main house.

FAQ

Can I do short-term rentals (Airbnb/VRBO)?
Many cities limit STRs in ADUs. Long-term (30+ days) leases are usually safer. Confirm your ordinance and zoning notes before marketing. {{verify locally}}
How often should I update my rent price?
Check comps monthly when vacant; during tenancy, follow any local cap/notice rules for increases and renewal timing. {{verify locally}}
Do I need a landlord registration or inspection?
Some cities require rental registrations, lead-safe certificates, or periodic inspections. Check city housing portals. {{verify locally}}
Should I include utilities?
Bundling utilities simplifies billing but hides usage. If shared meters, consider a fixed utility add-on. Separate meters improve fairness but add upfront cost.
What about pets?
Pet policies should be clear in the lease. Consider pet rent or deposit; follow fair-housing guidelines and service animal rules. {{verify locally}}
