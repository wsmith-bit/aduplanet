FAQ

Is ADUPlanet free to use?
Yes. Our planning guides are free. If you submit an RFQ, we may earn a referral fee from partners—never from marking up your costs.
How current is the regulatory information?
Each regulation page includes outbound links to official portals and a dateModified stamp. For city-level fees/timelines, look for {{verify locally}} notes that link to city sites.
Do you recommend specific builders?
We provide a directory and request details via /estimate to match you with suitable prefab or custom teams based on type, budget, and timeline.
Can you estimate my ADU cost?
Use the Cost Calculator on the Costs page for a realistic starting range, then refine with a builder RFQ.
Will an ADU raise my property taxes?
It depends on your state and local rules. See your state/city regulation page; we surface the relevant tax and assessment notes with official references.
How do you handle my data?
We use privacy-friendly analytics and only share RFQ details with matched partners. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
